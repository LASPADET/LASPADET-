About Us

LASPADET is the official body for phone and device technicians in Lagos State, Nigeria. Our mission is to unite technicians, promote skills, protect members’ interests, and provide quality service to the public.

Chairman: Com. Oseni Kabir (08053521459)
Secretary: Com. O.T. Mayowa (09073820002)

Our Activities

  • Technical training and workshops
  • Registration and certification of members
  • Partnership with telecom and device companies
  • Community service and youth empowerment

Membership

Open to all phone and device technicians in Lagos State.

Benefits include:

  • Skill training and upgrade
  • Networking and support
  • Recognition and protection
  • Business opportunities
